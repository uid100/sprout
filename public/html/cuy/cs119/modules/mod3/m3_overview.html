Chapter 3
Making Decisions

    Content:
  • Boolean Expressions and Decisions
  • Handling AND and OR
  • Range Selections

 

With chapter 3, we move into the second fundamental coding structure: Decision or selection structures. With these, we can take one of two possible paths of action depending on the result of some condition. The key takeaway is that our condition must evaluate to a true/false or yes/no result.

 

    At the end of this module, you will be able to:
  • Demonstrate the use of boolean expressions and operations
  • Demonstrate element selection within ranges
